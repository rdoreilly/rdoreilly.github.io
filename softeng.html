Software Engineering

My work in software engineering encompasses distributed systems and data-driven architectures. I have developed scalable, cloud-based systems for real-time data analytics and stream processing, which are essential for handling Big Data.

This architectural approach enables efficient knowledge extraction from vast datasets, supporting healthcare and IoT applications. Data visualisation plays a pivotal role in my work, where I emphasise intuitive, interactive designs to communicate complex insights effectively. I empower stakeholders to make informed decisions by integrating visual analytics into real-time systems, combining technical precision with user-centric design principles.
